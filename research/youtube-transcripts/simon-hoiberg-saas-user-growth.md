## Why This Video
Simon Høiberg breaks down exactly how content and YouTube fit into a SaaS growth roadmap — directly relevant to how B2B SaaS companies should use YouTube as an acquisition channel at different stages of growth.

## Key Takeaways
1. **Stage 1 (0-100 users):** Outgoing — reach out manually to your network, turn early users into advocates
2. **Stage 2 (100-1000 users):** Incoming via content — YouTube long-form, blog posts, TikTok/Reels, LinkedIn updates. This is where YouTube becomes critical.
3. **Stage 3 (1000+ users):** Paid ads + affiliate program — take your best content and scale it with paid distribution
4. **Content is the best incoming acquisition model** — it takes time but compounds over time
5. **YouTube specifically** — create long-form video content to show up on YouTube search, this is a core channel for SaaS growth
