## Why This Video
Adam Robinson scaled three SaaS companies to a combined $35M ARR. He covers founder brand content and YouTube as a core marketing strategy — directly relevant to how B2B SaaS founders should use content and video to drive inbound growth.

## Key Takeaways
1. **Founder brand content is #1 strategy** — LinkedIn audience scaled RB2B from $0 to $1M ARR in 6 weeks
2. **Be vulnerable, not polished** — sharing failures creates more engagement than sharing wins
3. **Content is ultimate leverage** — each post reaches 50-100K people, drives thousands of inbound leads monthly at zero cost
4. **Inbound-led outbound** — use content to drive website traffic, identify warm visitors (via RB2B), then outreach to them. 10x higher conversion than cold outreach
5. **UGC for B2B** — build community of creators around your product, incentivize with recognition + revenue opportunities
6. **Word of mouth > everything** — all marketing strategies work exponentially better once you have product-market fit
7. **Don't growth hack too early** — focus on product-market fit first, then layer in content/YouTube/ads
