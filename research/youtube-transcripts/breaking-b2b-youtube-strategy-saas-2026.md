## Key Takeaways
1. **Biggest mistake:** Using YouTube as a "video storage platform" — dumping ads and demo videos without strategy
2. **Optimize for leads, not views** — views are a vanity metric. The real metric is lead quantity and quality from your ICP
3. **YouTube builds trust better than any other channel** — long form + face + voice = highest converting content asset
4. **Video structure that works:**
   - Top of funnel: how-to/educational videos, 8-15 mins
   - Middle of funnel: deeper, longer, more in-depth
   - Bottom of funnel: case studies, client interviews — final push to convert
5. **Two CTAs per video** — one at 33% mark (soft, for warm audience), one at the end (direct, after value delivered)
6. **Thumbnail rules** — max 4 words, contrasting colors, big face, simple. Thumbnail = 60-70% of video performance
7. **YouTube feeds the entire marketing ecosystem** — repurpose to LinkedIn posts, newsletters, pre-sales call warm-up videos
